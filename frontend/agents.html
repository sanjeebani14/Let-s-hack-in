Five agents. One pipeline.

InRoad fixes discovery, entry, and proof — not keyword job boards. Every step runs on your real resume and projects.

FLOW

Profile in → Discovery → Matching → Chemistry → Proof → Outreach → Action package

Agent 1 — Opportunity Discovery

Scans founder posts, newsletters, communities, hackathons, and high-signal sources. Ranks by competition, response probability, growth, and referral likelihood.

Agent 2 — Relevance Matching

Builds a semantic skill profile from meaning behind your work — not keywords — and scores fit per opportunity.

Agent 3 — InRoad Chemistry

Maps your human path in: shared contexts, connectors, engagement signals, team fit. Produces the InRoad Score and your way in.

Agent 4 — Skill Proof

Evidence from projects: ownership, outcomes, complexity. Proof card per opportunity with confidence scores.

Agent 5 — Action & Outreach

Tactical next steps plus LinkedIn and email drafts attached to proof — ready to send.
